## Decision

Fixed buildIndexes so that the affects field contributes to the dependency graph. Previously, affects was stored on entries but never wired into dependencies.json, meaning query_deps was blind to half the relationship data agents provided. Now when an entry declares "X affects Y", it registers the same directed edge as "Y depends_on X" — specifically, Y appears in X's depended_on_by list, and X appears in Y's depends_on list. This makes the graph complete and query_deps reliable for blast-radius analysis.

## Risk

If affects and depends_on declare contradictory edges for the same module pair, both will be registered. There's no conflict resolution — the graph treats them as additive.
